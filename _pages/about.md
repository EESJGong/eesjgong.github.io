I am an M.S. student in Information and Communication Engineering at the School of Electronic and Information Engineering, South China University of Technology, advised by Prof. Shuangping Huang.

My research lies at the intersection of **3D generative modeling** and **LLM-driven structured generation**. So far, I have published first-author papers at CVPR 2025 and ICLR 2026, as well as a co-first-author paper at ECCV 2024, with research spanning 3D digital humans, neural rendering, and text-guided 3D/CAD synthesis. More broadly, I am interested in building generative systems that are not only capable, but also structured, controllable, and executable.

In 3D generation, my work has focused on digital humans and neural rendering, particularly **speech-driven 3D facial animation** and **Gaussian-based talking head generation**. Through this line of research, I study how representation design and motion modeling can improve realism, generalization, and rendering quality, while pushing toward high-fidelity 3D content generation.

In LLM-driven generation, I study how large models can move beyond free-form synthesis toward **structured, long-horizon, and executable generation**. My recent work on Text-to-CAD explores how natural language instructions can be translated into **hierarchical and geometry-aware graph representations**, which then guide CAD action generation and executable `bpy` code synthesis. To improve robustness on complex and highly constrained CAD assemblies, I further investigate **structure-aware curriculum learning** as a way to support progressive capability expansion and model self-improvement during training. More generally, I am interested in combining geometric priors, multimodal reasoning, and program-like intermediate representations to improve the reliability of complex generation systems.

Looking ahead, I am increasingly interested in a broader agenda: **physically grounded generative systems** that can reason over structure, interact with tools and environments, and improve through feedback. This motivates my current interests in **LLM agents**, **world models**, and **physically grounded 3D generation**. In the long run, I hope to build generative systems that can couple large-model reasoning with explicit structure, physical dynamics, and tool-based execution, enabling more reliable generation in complex open-ended environments.
